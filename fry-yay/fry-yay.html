Our Featured Local Business

Alfresh Cravings

If you are looking for delicious snacks but are worried about your health, you can try Alfresh's nutrient-dense, delicious treats. You can satisfy your cravings while staying healthy.

Fry-yay

Fry-yay is a take-out corner for your favorite silog meals. Silog meal is a local fastfood menu which is a combination of fried rice, eggs, and meat. They also offer other snacks like burgers and fries.

Our Purpose

Canaman Chamber of Commerce is created to promote our local business establishments, to post local events, news and weather updates. We also want to connect buyers and sellers within the community. This website also aims to attract tourists from other regions or even foreign countries.
We would like to help our local community to prosper. Everyone is welcome to visit and be a member of this website. You can check our join portal, if you want to be a member of our chamber.
